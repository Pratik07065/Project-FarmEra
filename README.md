🌾 Smart Farming Assistant

An AI-powered agriculture platform designed to help farmers make data-driven decisions through crop yield prediction, crop price forecasting, disease detection, and direct marketplace access.

🚀 Overview

Smart Farming Assistant combines Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and modern web technologies to address key challenges faced by farmers. The platform provides predictive insights, disease diagnosis, market intelligence, and direct buyer-seller communication to improve productivity and profitability.

🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers often struggle with:

- Uncertain crop yields
- Fluctuating market prices
- Crop diseases and pest attacks
- Limited access to buyers
- Lack of technical agricultural guidance

Smart Farming Assistant aims to solve these challenges through intelligent automation and predictive analytics.

---

✨ Key Features

🌱 Crop Yield Prediction

Predict expected crop production using historical agricultural and environmental data.

📈 Crop Price Prediction

Forecast future market prices using machine learning models to help farmers decide when to sell their produce.

🦠 Disease Detection

Identify crop diseases from images and provide treatment recommendations.

🤖 AI Agricultural Chatbot

Provide instant guidance on:

- Crop selection
- Fertilizer recommendations
- Disease management
- Farming best practices
- Government schemes

🛒 Farmer Marketplace

Enable direct interaction between farmers and buyers to reduce dependency on intermediaries.

📊 Data Analytics Dashboard

Visualize agricultural trends, production forecasts, and market insights.
